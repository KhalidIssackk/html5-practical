Why HTML5 Semantics Matter

The use of semantic elements greatly improves accessibility and search engine optimization. Modern browsers understand the meaning and purpose of each element, making content more discoverable and usable.

When documenting keyboard shortcuts, we use special tags. For example, press Ctrl + S to save your work, or Ctrl + P to print.
